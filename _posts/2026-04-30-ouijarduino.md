### My Circuitry

A cloth Ouija board controlled by the Arduino Lilypad, which lights up on “yes,” “no,” and “goodbye.” It plays eerie music through the buzzer when the light sensor detects darkness. A button is used to ask the question, get a yes or no answer, then press again for goodbye and once more to restart.
