### Major Oxymoron
The title of my current year at Williams is an oxymoron: sophomore is composed of the Ancient Greek σοφός (sophos, wise) and μωρός (moros, fool). 'Wise fool' is certainly how I navigate the world.

I hope to err more on the wise side for my Classics and Comparative Literature majors. My focus is Ancient Greek language and music.
